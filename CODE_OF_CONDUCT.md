# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project
a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that prioritize collaboration, curiosity,
and respect.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps create a positive environment include:

- showing empathy and patience, especially when discussing bugs or design tradeoffs
- giving and receiving constructive feedback in good faith
- being respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels
- focusing on what is best for the project and its community
- taking responsibility for mistakes and working toward repair when harm occurs

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary language
- insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- publishing someone else's private information without explicit permission
- sustained disruption of discussions, reviews, or issue threads
- sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including issues, pull
requests, code review, documentation, examples, and official community spaces.

It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in
public spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project
maintainers through a private channel if one is available to you.

If you do not yet have a private maintainer contact path, open a low-detail
issue requesting a private follow-up channel without including sensitive details
in public.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of
Conduct. They may take appropriate action in response to behavior they determine
is inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
